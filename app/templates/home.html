What is AI?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. This is the notion that technological devices such as computers can be trained to think, in approximately the same way that living creatures do. One way to test this concept, is that providing a device with a set of data, it can make decisions, outside of what it was coded to do.



How will AI impact me, and my gaming experience?

In short order, you could soon be playing games, with non-human components that are able to think, and perform actions, using logic that has not been hard-coded. This in turn will allow for the enemies in games to pose a greater challenge, allowing for a richer gaming experience. More information can be found on our How it works page.
